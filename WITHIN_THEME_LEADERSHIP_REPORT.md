### Insight 1: **Large-Cap Leadership is a 6-18 Month Leading Indicator**

Large-caps in a theme typically transition to bull regime 6-18 months before smaller-cap members. This is because:
- Large-caps have better information (management foresight)

- Large-caps attract institutional flows first
- Small-caps depend on large-cap capex and orders

**Investment Implication**: When you see large-cap leadership gap >60%, you have **6-18 months** to accumulate smaller-cap theme members before they catch up.
